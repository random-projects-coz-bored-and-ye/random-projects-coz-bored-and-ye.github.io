About Us

I started this hobby as a therapy tool for my PTSD, with the Resin Tumblers and Wine Glasses. I loved watching the tumblers on the turner and it relaxed me. After making so many I decided I had to sell some. So, this is how Hunter Resin Designs began. From there we have expanded into sublimation tumblers and wax melts and soon to be expanding again with Candles launching soon. (Watch this space) We put lots of love and careful thought into all we do. We hope you enjoy all we have to offer and share the experience with others. And a big thank you for supporting me with my Mental Health.
